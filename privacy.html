WHAT WE COLLECT
• Child's first name and chosen avatar (stored locally on this device)
• Anonymous progress: which problems were attempted, answer correctness, timing
• Coin / gem / streak totals (no real money, no personal info)

WHAT WE DO NOT COLLECT
• No real names, emails, phone numbers, addresses, or photos
• No GPS / precise location
• No microphone, camera, or contacts
• No personal advertising identifiers
• No behavioral tracking across other apps or websites

ADS (COPPA COMPLIANT)
• Only non-personalized, contextual ads (G-rated)
• All ad requests tagged as child-directed
• A parental gate is required before any ad is shown or any purchase is made
• You can remove ads forever with a one-time purchase

PURCHASES
• Processed by Apple App Store / Google Play
• We never see your credit card or billing info
• All purchases require parental verification

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY (COPPA / GDPR-K)
We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) in the US
and GDPR-K in the EU. We do not knowingly collect personal information from
children under 13 beyond what is necessary to operate the app.

DATA STORAGE
Progress data is stored on a secure server using anonymous identifiers. No
data is sold or shared with advertisers or third parties.

YOUR RIGHTS
You can delete all data at any time from the Parent Dashboard → Reset Profile.
